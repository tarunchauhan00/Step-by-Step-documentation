    How Users Can Use It¶

    These instructions explain, in plain steps, how an Owner/Admin and a Doer/Contributor actually interact with the running application.

    1. Owner/Admin Workflow¶

    1. Sign Up / Log In
    2. Navigate to http://localhost:3000/auth.
    3. If you haven’t signed up, click Sign Up and fill in email/password. Make sure your Supabase user record has role = owner.

    4. After you sign up, you’ll land on /processes once you log in.

    5. View Processes

    6. On /processes, you’ll see all the Processes you own.

    7. If there are none yet, an empty-state message appears plus a button Add Process.

    8. Create a Process

    9. Click Add Process → fill in “Title” and “Description” → click Save.

    10. You’ll be redirected back to /processes, and your new Process card will show up.

    11. Open a Process

    12. Click on the Process card → /process/<processId>/steps (StepCreation).

    13. You’ll see an “Add Step” button since no steps exist yet.

    14. Add Steps

    15. Click Add Step → fill:
      • Step Title and Description
      • Due Date (e.g. 2025-06-15)
      • Assign to Doer (choose from dropdown; your Doers must already have signed up as role = doer)
      • (Optional) Google Form URL if you want them to fill out a Google Form.
      • (Optional) Custom Form (if you previously created a form under “Forms”).

    16. Click Save. That Step now appears in the list.

    17. Bulk Edit Steps

    18. If you have multiple steps, click Bulk Edit → checkboxes appear next to each step.

    19. Select several, choose a new “Due Date” or new “Assigned Doer” at the top, then click Apply. All selected steps update.

    20. Create/Manage Custom Forms (optional)

    21. Navigate to “Forms” in the Navbar (/forms).
    22. Click Add Form, enter a Form Name and Description, click Next → “FormBuilder.”
    23. Drag or click to add fields (text, number, date, dropdown).

    24. Give each field a label, mark “Required” if needed, and click Save. A new formId is generated.

    25. Link a Custom Form to a Step

    26. When creating or editing a Step, select your custom form from the “Custom Form” dropdown.

    27. Now, when a Doer goes to fill that step, they see the custom form instead of a Google Form.

    28. Grant Ownership (optional)

    29. In the Navbar, click Grant Ownership → a dropdown of other users appears.
    30. Select the new owner (they must already exist with role = owner in Supabase).

    31. Click Transfer. All your processes are now owned by the new user, and you will lose admin rights (unless you repeat the process).

    32. Send Reminders

      • Navigate to Reminders in the Navbar (/reminders).
      • You’ll see steps that are “Due Soon” or “Overdue.”
      • Click Send Reminder next to any step—this triggers an email (if your Supabase SMTP is configured) or logs a reminder in the database.

    33. Sync Google Form Responses

      • After linking a Google Form to a Step, you need to sync responses.
      • Go to Sync Responses (/sync-responses).
      • Click Sync All (or sync them individually). You’ll see messages like “Fetched 3 new responses for Form ID: abc123.”
      • Those answers now live in Supabase’s form_responses table.

    34. View Reports

      • Click Reports in the Navbar (/report).
      • You’ll see charts/tables for:
      • Percentage of Steps Completed vs. Pending
      • Doer-wise completion counts (e.g., John: 15/20, Jane: 12/18)
      • List of Overdue Steps
      • Use the filters (by Process, by Doer, by date) to customize what you see.

    35. Log Out

      • In the Navbar, click Log Out. You’ll return to /auth.

    2. Doer/Contributor Workflow¶

    1. Sign Up / Log In
    2. Go to /auth → click Sign Up → fill in email/password → assign yourself role = doer in Supabase (or have an admin do it).

    3. After logging in, you’ll land on a Doer dashboard or be redirected to /doer/steps.

    4. View Assigned Steps

    5. On /doer/steps, see a list of all pending/in‐progress steps assigned to you, with due dates.

    6. Open a Step

    7. Click a step → /doer/step/<stepId>.
    8. If the step has a custom form, you’ll see inputs rendered by FormSchemaRenderer.

    9. If it’s a Google Form, an iframe of the form appears.

    10. Fill Out & Submit

    11. Complete the required fields and click Submit.
    12. The app saves your answers into form_responses.
    13. That step’s status updates to “Completed” in Supabase.

    14. You’re redirected to /doer/submission/<submissionId>, where you can view what you submitted.

    15. View Past Submissions

    16. On /doer/steps, switch to the “Completed” filter.

    17. Click any completed step to see your submission details (read-only).

    18. Receive Reminders

    19. If an admin sends you a reminder, you’ll either:

      • Get an email (if SMTP is configured), or
      • See an in-app alert (if they used the ReminderSection page).

    20. Log Out

    21. Click Log Out in the Navbar → back to /auth.
